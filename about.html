Experience

Since 2017, I've been working at HOV, a remote-first product studio/venture studio/accelerator, as Lead Designer.

Before that, I’ve spent 10+ years as a freelancer and consultant, using words, pictures and code to help out clients all over the world. I had a chance to work on a colorful selection of projects—from L.A. rappers and Dutch apps for kids, to Croatian funeral a cappella choirs and Yale academics. It was a blast, I met a lot of brilliant people and learned the upsides and downsides of working remotely.

I’m still accepting short-term projects, but what I’m really looking for is a group of awesome people creating smart, easy-to-use digital products or services. If this sounds like you and you’d like to have a chat about working together, send me an email.
