High Output Ventures

From 2017 to 2021 I worked as a lead designer for High Output Ventures, a startup studio and accelerator. Part of my job was defining and evolving the brand of High Output Ventures as it grew.

I've also helped numerous domain experts, indie creators, and founders build their products from 0 to 1. I helped them position themselves through branding sprints and shape their ideas, knowledge and workflows into successful web and mobile products.

Identifi is a tool that helps managers and direct reports build stronger connections through regular 1-on-1s. I've designed the web and mobile apps, a marketing site, and made dozens of illustrations and icons for the product.

I helped Tam Ging Wien establish his brand and scale his business through REITScreener. His blog and seminars on making smarter decisions when investing in REITs proved very popular, so we worked together to build an online platform which combined online courses, news, screeners, and other educational content, which ended up being used by thousands of users.

For all of these products, I'd create simple but thorough style guides in Sketch, which helped our team easily build new features, site pages, social network content and other assets.
