Work⤵

2021—2025

Ghost

Senior product designer

An independent, non-profit organization building open-source tools for writers, journalists and publishers.

Over the past year, I worked as lead designer on bringing social web features to Ghost. This turned Ghost publications into networked social profiles that people can find, follow, like, reply, repost and interact with, not just from Ghost, but also WordPress, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon and others. bringing network effects to generate growth, and reaching new people.

We worked fast, shipping updates every week and iterating based on feedback from our passionate beta users. If you're curious, you can read the entire journey in the changelog archive.

Before that, I worked on a dozen of features that help people: growth analytics2, improved onboarding3, two-factor authentication3, email notifications, rich media editor cards, and many more.

I've also led design on rebuilding internal tools, which took a cumbersome command-line interface and turned it into a smooth, responsive web-app, allowing Ghost's customer support team to help people in a faster and easier way.

Ghost is used by thousands of amazing journalists, writers and bloggers. It feels really good seeing how folks like 404 Media are using it for important journalism, or how seasoned blogger and Stack Overflow co-founder Jeff Atwood chose it for his blog.

I've learned a lot during my time at Ghost and I'll really miss the sweet and talented group working there. And Ghost is still the best tool to publish content online if you ask me—give it a try.
