Helping beginners invest in REITs

My role: UX, UI, Branding, Prototyping

I worked with Tam Ging Wien, a seasoned REITs expert and educator, on helping people invest in REITs.

We’ve built REITScreener, a powerful tool which helps you access learning resources, stay up to date with the market and analyze your wins and losses.

REITs are a tricky topic, so we had to create a smooth funnel that would lead a rookie from free webinars to the point where they’re using pro features, and I was in charge of designing it.

I’m no longer working on REITScreener, but it’s now a profitable product, used by hundreds of people every day.

Connecting creators with their fans

My role: UX, UI, IA, Branding, Prototyping

I designed Jamclout, a platform where creators can connect with fans through crypto.

Creators can earn through tips, NFTs and social coins, and fans can get access to their exclusive posts, support them and literally invest in them if they believe they’re going to become big.

My role covered everything from shaping the product through user story mapping sessions, to creating a simple design system to make the UI consistent.

Jamclout is currently in closed beta, and we’re working with creators on improving it daily.
