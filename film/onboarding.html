How to use

  1. Borko gives you a random movie from one or more Letterboxd watchlists
  2. Enter one or more Letterboxd usernames
  3. Decide if you want something that’s in everybodies watchlist (intersect) or in at least one watchlist (union)
  4. Get a recommendation from the only algorithm you can always trust: randomized
