HTML Day 2025: Lisbon, Portugal

Saturday, August 2nd
15:00 - 18:00
Parque Eduardo VII

What is this?

Every year, people around the world get together to hang out and write HTML for HTML Day.

What should I bring?

A (charged) laptop and yourself. Optionally: a picnic blanket, some snacks, and a friend!

Where exactly?

Parque Eduardo VII, Lisbon, Portugal.

Do I have to be good at HTML?

Nope, everyone is welcome, and we'll help each other out!

What should I make?

Maybe a website for your dog? or favorite recipe? or favorite song/poem/movie/artist? entirely up to you!
